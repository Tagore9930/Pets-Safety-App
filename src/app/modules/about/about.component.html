Tagore Banda

In 1996, before I thought of becoming a dog trainer, I have adopted an 8 month old golden retriever named Cherry. Cherry ate couches, sheetrock, jumped on people, etc. Since I knew I needed help, so I have called a dog trainer to come to my home. Not only did she train Cherry, she trained me too. I have learned a lot of thing to do in terms of dog training. I was so excited that I could train Cherry, then I quit my job and start to get some education about dog training. I've been training people and their dogs ever since. Here is my certification list:

Certified Veterinary Technician

Certified Canine Athlete Specialist (CCAS)

Certified Dog Trainer (CDT)

Certified Dog Trainer Advanced (CDTA)

Professional Dog Trainer Instructor (PDTI)

Certified Service Dog Trainer (CSDT)
